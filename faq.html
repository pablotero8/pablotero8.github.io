Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our service.

How can I start my online training?

The first thing you should do is contact us via email, through our social media, or via WhatsApp.

The next step will be a video call where we will show you our way of working, set the goals you want to achieve, and explain everything in depth!

If I have limited equipment and little time, can I still train?

The great and beautiful thing about the training world is that there are a thousand ways to train created to fit everyone's needs.

What time of the day should I do my workouts?

Whenever you want! The great thing about online training is that once the workouts are created, you decide when to train during the day.
