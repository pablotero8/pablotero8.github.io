About DMM Trainer

What sets DMM Trainer apart from other online personal trainers, especially in the world of racquet sports?

Training by Quarters, Progress Through Structure

I work in three-month cycles because progress requires structure, follow-up, and time. Each trimester is carefully planned to help you advance measurably and consistently, with clear objectives and personalized strategies. It's scientifically proven that 12 weeks of planned training is necessary for significant changes in our bodies. I don't believe in quick fixes or generic programs—I believe in commitment, evolution, and continuous improvement.

Specialized in Racquet Sports

Being specialized in racquet sports means I understand not only the physical demands of these sports but also the technique, injury prevention, and specific preparation you need to perform at your best on the court. Whether you're an amateur or a competitive player, I'll adapt your training to enhance your performance and keep you fit throughout the season.

My Training Philosophy

Training with me is not just training—it's having a plan, a guide, and a real commitment to your improvement. My training philosophy is based on injury prevention and eliminating pain as the main objective. If there's an injury, it doesn't matter how fast or strong you are, because you won't be able to compete or train effectively.

My Credentials and Experience

  • Bachelor's Degree in Physical Activity and Sports Sciences, Specialized in Racquet Sports
  • Former High-Performance Athlete (7 years)
  • Tennis and Padel Coach (5+ years of experience)
  • Personal Trainer (3+ years of experience)

Who I've Trained

I've worked with all types of people: tennis players, padel players, seniors, people with illnesses and in recovery, National Police Academy candidates, and people who simply wanted to improve their physical fitness.
