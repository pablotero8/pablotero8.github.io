Who we are????

Dani & Erika

Daniel & Erika have over 10 years of expertise in elite fitness, nutrition, and Michelin-starred cuisine to create a refined, results-driven wellness experience.

- Erika is an ISSA-certified personal trainer and nutritionist with more than a decade of experience working with high-profile clients, executives, and public figures. Her work focuses on precision training and strategic nutrition tailored to the most demanding lifestyles.

-Daniel is a professional chef trained in Michelin-starred kitchens, with over ten years of experience delivering high-end culinary solutions designed to support performance, health, and longevity.

Together they created PAODAN HABITS, where they offer a bespoke wellness service where training, nutrition, and fine dining merge seamlessly to elevate performance, well-being, and lifestyle at the highest level.

Our Services

Choose the wellness plan that best fits your lifestyle and goals.

Digital Coaching Program

Complete remote wellness solutions designed for the modern professional. Our expert trainers and nutritionists create a personalized training regimen and meal plan tailored to your specific goals. You maintain complete flexibility—our professionals work with you remotely, eliminating the need for in-home visits while delivering elite-level results from anywhere.

Hybrid Wellness Program

The perfect balance of convenience and personal attention. Combine remote coaching sessions with in-person training at your preferred location. You choose the frequency and distribution of sessions—whether it's mostly remote with occasional in-home visits, or vice versa. Enjoy the flexibility to adapt your plan as your schedule evolves throughout the year.

Premium In-Residence Experience

Our most comprehensive offering for those seeking complete immersion. Receive personalized training sessions in your home or preferred gym facility, combined with our signature private chef service. Your dedicated chef prepares gourmet meals specifically designed to complement your training program, ensuring optimal nutrition and recovery. True wellness excellence at your residence.

Programs are fully customizable to your schedule. We design solutions that fit seamlessly into demanding lifestyles.
